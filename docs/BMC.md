# Business Model Canvas: private-guard

## Value Proposition
Private-Guard offers a privacy-first alternative to mainstream digital tools and services by eliminating ads, tracking, and data collection. It provides users with secure, transparent, and user-controlled access to essential online functionalities without compromising personal information or browsing experience.

## Customer Segments
- Privacy-conscious individuals seeking secure alternatives to mainstream platforms
- Tech-savvy users who prioritize data protection and digital sovereignty
- Small businesses requiring compliant, non-tracking solutions for internal operations
- Organizations aiming to reduce liability from third-party data exposure

## Channels
- Direct download from GitHub repository (`arkashira/surrogate-1-harvest`)
- Community forums and open-source platforms (e.g., Lemmy, Reddit)
- Partner ecosystems within the Axentx swarm for cross-promotion
- Developer documentation and integration guides hosted on the company's knowledge base (pgvector-based)

## Revenue Streams
- Freemium model with optional premium subscriptions for advanced features
- Licensing fees for enterprise-grade deployments within partner organizations
- Whitelabel partnerships offering branded versions for specific verticals
- Support contracts for custom integrations and compliance audits

## Cost Structure
- Development and maintenance of core software stack using verified frameworks (vLLM, SGLang)
- Infrastructure hosting and scaling costs managed through Axentx’s autonomous infrastructure
- Human resources for product management, engineering, QA, and support roles
- Legal and compliance overhead related to privacy certifications and licensing

- Company brain (pgvector) containing all product knowledge, datasets, and historical context

- Community-driven feedback loop ensuring continuous improvement aligned with user needs

- Continuous development and enhancement of privacy-focused tools based on validated market signals
- Integration of new features derived from real-world usage patterns and feedback

- Collaboration with other Axentx agents to ensure alignment with existing portfolio offerings
- Regular updates to comply with evolving privacy regulations and industry best practices
