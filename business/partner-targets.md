## Partner Integration Roadmap for Private Guard

### Target Integrations

1. **DuckDuckGo**
   - **Rationale**: A privacy-focused search engine that aligns with the values of Private Guard. Integrating DuckDuckGo can enhance user experience by providing ad-free, tracking-free search results.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited searches.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides users with a privacy-centric search tool, enhancing their online privacy experience.

2. **ProtonMail**
   - **Rationale**: A secure email service that emphasizes privacy and security. Integrating ProtonMail allows users to communicate without the fear of tracking or data mining.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 500 MB storage, limited features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates secure communication for users, addressing their need for privacy in email correspondence.

3. **Signal**
   - **Rationale**: A messaging app that offers end-to-end encryption and prioritizes user privacy. Integrating Signal can provide users with a secure communication option.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited messaging and calls.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances user privacy in messaging, solving the job of secure communication.

4. **Ghostery**
   - **Rationale**: A privacy extension that blocks ads and trackers. Integrating Ghostery can enhance the Private Guard experience by providing additional tracking protection.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Basic ad-blocking features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances user privacy by blocking unwanted ads and trackers, solving the job of maintaining online anonymity.

5. **Tutanota**
   - **Rationale**: Another secure email service that offers end-to-end encryption. Integrating Tutanota can provide users with more options for secure email communication.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 1 GB storage, limited features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Offers users a choice in secure email services, addressing their need for privacy.

6. **Startpage**
   - **Rationale**: A search engine that allows users to search the web anonymously. Integrating Startpage can provide an alternative search option that prioritizes privacy.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited searches.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides users with a privacy-focused search alternative, enhancing their overall privacy experience.

7. **1Password**
   - **Rationale**: A password manager that helps users manage their passwords securely. Integrating 1Password can enhance user security and privacy by managing sensitive information.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Limited features for free users.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of securely managing passwords, enhancing user privacy.

8. **Bitwarden**
   - **Rationale**: An open-source password manager that emphasizes security and privacy. Integrating Bitwarden can provide users with a secure way to manage their passwords.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited passwords, basic features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Addresses the need for secure password management, enhancing user privacy.

### Summary
The proposed integrations focus on enhancing the privacy and security of users while providing additional value through affiliate/revenue-share opportunities. Each integration has been evaluated based on its relevance to the Private Guard product, free-tier limits, integration effort, and the specific user jobs it addresses.
